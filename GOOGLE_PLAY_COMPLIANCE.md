## 📋 Required Metadata for Store Submission

### App Title
**AI Study Planner**

### Short Description
Organize your study tasks, track progress, and get AI-powered help with your homework.

### Full Description
AI Study Planner is a comprehensive study management app designed to help students stay organized and productive. Features include:

- **Task Management**: Create, organize, and track study tasks with priority levels and due dates
- **Calendar View**: Visualize your tasks and deadlines on an interactive calendar
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Use the scientifically-proven Pomodoro technique with customizable work and break intervals
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your study streaks, completed tasks, and study time with detailed analytics
- **AI Study Assistant**: Get instant answers to your questions with our AI Chat Bot
- **Question Solver**: Upload photos of questions and get step-by-step solutions
- **Marking Scheme**: Get detailed evaluations and marking schemes for your answers
- **Subject Organization**: Organize your tasks by subject or course

All data is stored locally on your device. Optional rewarded ads allow you to unlock additional AI features.
